Introduction

My name is Eshani. I wear many hats depending on what is needed, a teacher, architect, mother, wife, daughter, aunt and student. I love art and am passionate about teaching. Every few years I try to learn something new and reinvent myself. Currently I am at that phase of life. I love to paint, sketch and craft. I experiment with new recipes even if some are rather disastrous. I love to travel with my family.

Education and Career Goals

I started out with a degree in architecture and worked for many years as an architect and interior designer. When I volunteered at my daughter's school, I realized I love being around little children so I went back to school and got my Master Teacher certificate. I have been teaching for the past 10 years. Here I am now, back in school learning some coding and web designing. My goal is to earn a degree in Web design and get a job that allows me to use my design skills and spend time with my family at the same time.
